Cheeseburger:

Description:

100% 1/4lb Beef patties

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Buns

Steps:

  1. Place patties on grill for 3 minutes then flip.
  2. After 2 minutes place cheese on patties. Wait 1 minute.
  3. Place patties on bun. Add lettuce & tomato.
