Description:

Pepperoni Pizza with Mozzarella cheese.

Oven-baked for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Dough
  • Pepperoni

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Roll the dough.
  3. Add the sauce to the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the cheese
  5. Add the Pepperoni
  6. Put in the over for 18-21 minutes.
